About Us

Your Trusted
Sourcing Partner

MAK ASIA BD is a dynamic and reliable garments buying house based in Bangladesh, specializing in sourcing, product development, and production management for international clients.

We act as a bridge between global buyers and local manufacturers, ensuring quality, cost efficiency, and timely delivery. Our team brings deep industry knowledge and commitment to every partnership.
